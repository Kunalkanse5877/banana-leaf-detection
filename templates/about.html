About the Crop Disease Detection Project

This project uses advanced machine learning algorithms to detect diseases in crops, providing farmers with essential insights on how to manage and treat their crops.

Our mission is to help farmers identify diseases early and take preventive measures before their crops are severely affected, reducing losses and improving agricultural productivity.
